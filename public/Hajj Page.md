## **How Hajj Booking Works (Nusuk Only)**   
* Pilgrims from serviced countries must register, select their Hajj package, and pay  **directly**  on the official Nusuk Hajj platform; this is the only approved digital system for Hajj bookings under the Direct Hajj Program.   
* Inside Nusuk, you create an account, upload your documents, browse approved Hajj packages from licensed Saudi Hajj service providers, and complete secure online payment.   
* For those who wish to travel  **with Naasir Travel’s guided group** , you will need to choose the specific Hajj service provider and package that we indicate inside Nusuk, so your booking is linked to the same provider we are working with.   
Naasir Travel will  **never**  take payment for Hajj packages; your booking and funds stay within Nusuk and with the authorized Saudi Hajj service provider you select.

## **What You Can Expect with Naasir Travel**   
When you register for Hajj 2027 guidance with Naasir Travel, you can expect:   
* **Full Nusuk support:**  Clear, step‑by‑step guidance during the key Nusuk phases—from registration and profile setup, to package selection and purchase, and up to receiving your visa and tickets—while you remain in control of your own account.   
* **Correct package linking:**  Help understanding Nusuk package options and selecting the specific service provider/package required to be part of our guided group (you still click and pay within your Nusuk account).   
* **Pre‑Hajj workshop (after Ramadan):**  One structured workshop before you travel, covering Hajj rituals, health and safety, packing, and realistic day‑by‑day schedules for Hajj 2027, based on the latest guidance from our partnered Saudi Hajj service provider.   
* **Hajj 2027 WhatsApp group:**  A dedicated group chat for our pilgrims, which serves as the main channel for schedules, meeting points, updates, reminders, and Q&A before departure and throughout the days of Hajj.   
* **Direct line to the Hajj service provider:**  We maintain direct communication with our selected Saudi Hajj service provider, so we can relay instructions, timing changes, and logistical updates to you quickly and clearly.   
* **On‑the‑ground group guidance:**  Coordination and reminders during the key days of Hajj, while all transfers and logistics are organized by the Hajj service provider; we receive their timings (for example, when buses are expected to be ready and depart) and relay those real‑time updates so you know what to do and where to be.   
* **Realistic expectations:**  Honest, transparent communication when there are schedule changes, delays, or limitations outside our control (for example, traffic congestion, crowd management plans, or operational decisions by Saudi authorities or your Hajj service provider).   
* **Local team support:**  A team based in Richmond, BC, that understands travel logistics, documentation, and time zones, and supports pilgrims wherever they are travelling from (as long as Nusuk allows them to book with our chosen provider).   
* **Important:**  Naasir Travel is  **not**  a Hajj service provider and does  **not**  control or guarantee the services delivered during Hajj (such as buses, accommodation, meals, or on‑site facilities). These are fully managed by your Saudi Hajj service provider through your Nusuk booking and are subject to local regulations and conditions. Naasir Travel serves only as a guide and group coordinator and does not receive your Hajj package payments. Your Hajj package is paid only through Nusuk; optional add‑on services such as qurbani may be arranged and paid through Naasir Travel separately.

## **What We Kindly Expect from You**   
To keep the group organized, safe, and fairly served, we have a simple code of conduct for our Hajj guidance:   
* **Be clear about your intention:**  Let us know if you are just learning about Hajj or actively planning to go in the upcoming season, so we can support you appropriately.   
* **Join our group if you rely on us:**  If you are actively preparing for Hajj and relying on our ongoing guidance over many weeks or months, we kindly expect that you will join Naasir Travel’s guided group and select the Nusuk Hajj service provider we are working with, so we can support you properly from start to finish. If you decide to travel with a different company or service provider, please let us know as early as possible and understand that our detailed one‑to‑one support and real‑time coordination are reserved primarily for those travelling with our group.   
* **Fair use of our guidance:**  Your choice of Nusuk provider remains entirely your own, but our detailed and ongoing guidance is designed for pilgrims who are part of Naasir Travel’s guided group. If you make extensive use of our support over many months and then choose to book with another provider, please understand that we may limit further one‑to‑one assistance so that we can focus our limited time and energy on the pilgrims we are directly responsible for.   
* **Respectful use of our time (we are not 24/7):**  Hajj is not the only group we serve and we also manage our day‑to‑day travel operations, so our team is not available around the clock. Please read all information we share (messages, handbook, and workshop materials), as it will answer most common questions. If you do message us, kindly avoid expecting instant replies; we will respond as soon as we reasonably can during our working hours, and if your question has already been addressed, we may refer you back to the previous answer or relevant resource rather than repeating it in full. We appreciate your understanding.   
* **Use channels wisely:**  Whenever possible, keep general questions for group sessions or the WhatsApp group, and reserve one‑to‑one questions for matters that genuinely need personal attention.   
* **Follow instructions during Hajj:**  Respect group timings, meeting points, and instructions during Hajj, recognizing that movements are tightly controlled by Saudi authorities and fixed transport schedules.   
* **Take care of your health:**  Take responsibility for your own health and fitness by consulting your doctor, managing medications, and preparing for heat, walking, and crowds.   
* **Maintain patience, respect, and adab:**  Treat fellow pilgrims, Naasir Travel staff, and service‑provider personnel with patience and respect, especially in stressful moments.
